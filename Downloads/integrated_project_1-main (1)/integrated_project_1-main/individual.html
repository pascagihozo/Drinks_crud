Q&A with Samantha Kelosi

Here we have Samantha, a Zimbabwean girl who is at some point not aware what she is doing at African Leadership College. She thinks that she wassn't supposed to be here at this moment. Let's see what she has to tell us about her whole ALC experience so far.

Where have you studied before coming to ALU?

Samantha: I studied in Harare, Zimbabwe, a school called Crazy Minds High School.

How did you hear about ALU?

Samantha: I had this one friend of mine who just called me one day and we had been talking about where we are going to study next after our high school......
